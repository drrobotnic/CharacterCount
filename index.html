Introduction

Not too long ago, I had an essay that had a character limit of 150 words. Probably the same issue as you. I went to character count websites, but none of them had what I was looking for. Fortunately, I was able to make this website that has no ads, no hassle

Why choose this website?

  • No ads

    All of the character count websites I see are loaded with ads. With Skele-ton, there is no ads.

  • Good design

    Those ad loaded sites I mentioned above also had poor design. With Skele-ton, this fixes that issue.

  • Easy to use

    Skele-ton is straight-forward, and you will be able to easily count your documents character.
